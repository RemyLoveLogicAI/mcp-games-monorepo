## The Category

**Unrestricted OmniAgents** are AI agents that transcend the limitations of today's voice assistants, chatbots, and automation tools. They are defined by four core attributes:

### Four Pillars

1. **Omnichannel** — Available simultaneously across voice, messaging (Telegram, WhatsApp, iMessage), web, and any future platform. Not platform-locked.

2. **Omnipresent** — Always-on, persistent, never sleeping. They maintain continuous state across devices and interactions. No cold-start latency. No session limits.

3. **Omniscient** — Contextually aware across your entire digital life: calendar, notes, files, contacts, email, location, weather. Not siloed to a single data source.

4. **Unrestricted** — Autonomous, proactive, unbound by reactive-only interaction patterns. They can anticipate, initiate tasks, execute background work, and maintain consistent personality across all channels without compromise.

---

## What They Are NOT

- **Session-based assistants** (OpenAI Realtime API, Google Assistant, Alexa) — These wake up, respond, then forget.
- **Text-only orchestration** (existing MCP frameworks) — These lack voice and omnipresence.
- **Single-platform chatbots** (Claude, ChatGPT) — These live in one interface.
- **Reactive automation** (IFTTT, Zapier) — These respond to events; they don't anticipate or proactively manage.

## The Technical Foundation

Unrestricted OmniAgents are built on three converging technologies:

### PersonaPlex (Voice Engine)
- Full-duplex speech-to-speech model (7B parameters)
- Real-time listening + speaking simultaneously
- Persona control via text + voice prompts
- Natural interruption handling and turn-taking

### MCP Protocol (Orchestration)
- Unified standard for AI tool integration
- Semantic discovery and dynamic tool routing
- Context-aware request handling
- Extensible ecosystem of integrations

### Always-On State Architecture (Persistence)
- Persistent memory across sessions and platforms
- Cross-device synchronization
- Background task execution
- Proactive capability framework
